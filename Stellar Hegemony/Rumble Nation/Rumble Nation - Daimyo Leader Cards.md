# Rumble Nation - Daimyo / Leader Cards

**Source:** Rumble_Nation_Cards_EN_2.2.pdf

## Daimyo Cards (Special Leader Effects)

These are powerful one-time effects tied to specific historical Daimyo.

### Daimyo Effects

- **Uesugi Kenshin**  
  Move up to 3 of your ARMIES from the area where your Daimyo is located to 1 or more adjacent areas CONNECTED BY LAND.

- **Oda Nobunaga**  
  Move your Daimyo to an adjacent area. You may move 1 ARMY in your DAIMYO‘s current area with HIM to the new area.

- **Oda Nobunaga (alternative)**  
  Move your Daimyo UP to 2 areas, AS LONG as you have armies in the area he passes through. You may move 1 ARMY in your DAIMYO‘s current area with HIM to the new area.  
  *(Note: Alternate version — original seems underpowered compared to others.)*

- **Chōsokabe Motochika**  
  Move up to 3 of your units (ARMIES or Daimyo) from the area where your Daimyo is located to 1 or more areas CONNECTED BY SEA.

- **Takeda Shingen**  
  Place 1 ARMY from your stock IN an area THAT IS adjacent to where your Daimyo is located. Do this up to 3 times IN up to 3 different areas (max. 1 army per Area).

- **Mōri Motonari**  
  REPLACE 1 enemy ARMY from UP TO 2 areas adjacent to your Daimyo WITH AN ARMY from your stock. RETURN THE ARMIES YOU REPLACED TO THE STOCK OF THEIR OWNER(S).

- **Ōtomo Sōrin**  
  Move up to 3 ARMIES OF THE SAME OPPONENT from the area where your Daimyo is located to 1 or more adjacent areas.
