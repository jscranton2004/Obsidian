# Rumble Nation - Rules Overview

**Source:** Rumble_Nation_Deluxe_EN_2.1.pdf

## Game Concept
You are warlords during the Sengoku Era (Civil War Japan). The goal is to achieve supremacy by contending for 11 Castles through area control.

## Components
- 72 Armies (18 per color: red, blue, yellow, black)
- 1 Map Board (11 areas)
- 30 white Reinforcements
- 11 Large Castle Tokens (values 2–12)
- 11 Small Castle Tokens (values 1–6)
- 4 Sword Tokens (values 4-3-2-1)
- 12 Dice (3 per color)
- 12 Tactic Cards
- 6 Daimyo Cards (Leaders)
- 4 Daimyos (one per color)

## Game Phases

### Phase I: Deployment Phase
- Players take turns deploying armies or using Tactic Cards.
- On your turn you must either:
  1. **Deploy Armies** (roll 3 dice, split into 2+1, place based on sums)
  2. **Use a Tactic Card** (once per game only)

**Deployment Rules:**
- Roll 3 dice, may reroll all once.
- Split into a 2-dice group and 1 single die.
- Place armies on the area matching the 2-dice sum.
- Number of armies placed = value on the single die.
- Must place all armies unless stock is empty.

### Phase II: War Phase
- Resolve wars area by area (lowest castle value first).
- Count armies + reinforcements = power.
- Highest power wins the Large Castle Token.
- Runner-up takes Small Castle Token (in 3-4 player games).
- Winner places reinforcements in adjacent areas.

## End Game
- Add up values on all Castle Tokens you control.
- Highest total wins (tiebreaker = higher Sword Token).

---

**Notes for Stellar Hegemony:**
- This is fundamentally an area control + dice deployment game with powerful one-time Tactic/Leader effects.
- The core tension comes from the Deployment Phase decisions and the powerful but limited Tactic Cards.
