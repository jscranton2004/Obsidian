# Rumble Nation - Tactic Cards

**Source:** Rumble_Nation_Cards_EN_2.2.pdf

## Tactic Cards (Deployment Phase)

These cards can be used **once per game** during the Deployment Phase instead of deploying armies.

### Movement Cards

- **Scout**  
  Move 1 of your ARMIES to an adjacent area.

- **Over The Mountain**  
  Move 2 of your ARMIES from 1 area to an adjacent area that is connected by land.

- **MARINE**  
  Move 2 of your ARMIES from 1 area to an adjacent area that is connected by SEA.

- **SUB SQUAD**  
  Move ½ of your ARMIES (rounded down) from 1 area to an adjacent area.

- **Ninja**  
  Move 1 of your ARMIES to any area where you have ARMIES.

- **Entering Kyo**  
  Move any number of your ARMIES to 京 (Kyo), from 1+ area(s) adjacent to it.

### Support & Disruption Cards

- **Support**  
  PLACE 1 ARMY from your stock IN any area where you have ARMIES.

- **Rebel**  
  Return 1 enemy ARMY to their stock & replace it with AN ARMY from your stock.

- **Exile**  
  Move 1 enemy ARMY from an area where you have ARMIES to an adjacent area.

- **Prisoner**  
  Move 1 enemy ARMY from any area to an adjacent area where you have ARMIES.

- **Decoy**  
  Move 1 of your ARMIES with 2 ARMIES of an enemy to an adjacent area.

- **Retreat**  
  Return 2 of your ARMIES from any area(s) to your stock.
