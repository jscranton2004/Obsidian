# Stellar Hegemony - Adapted Flag Ship Cards

**Last Updated:** 2026-05-28
**Source:** Rumble Nation Daimyo Cards (mechanics preserved exactly)

**Important Changes:**
- "Daimyo" → **Flag Ship**
- Only the stronger **Oda Nobunaga (alternative)** version is used
- Effects are rethemed for space, but **mechanical effects remain identical**

---

## Flag Ship Cards

**Commander Skitter** (Ant-men) – *The Swarm Lord*  
**Effect:** Move up to 3 of your FLEETS from the system where your Flag Ship is located to 1 or more adjacent systems **via hyperspace lanes**.

**Admiral Vance "Ironjaw" Hale** (Human) – *The Last Terran*  
**Effect:** Move your Flag Ship up to 2 systems, **as long as you have fleets in the system it passes through**. You may move 1 FLEET in your Flag Ship’s current system with it to the new system.

**High Lord Glubthar** (Fish-men) – *Voice of the Abyssal Current*  
**Effect:** Move up to 3 of your units (FLEETS or Flag Ship) from the system where your Flag Ship is located to 1 or more systems **via wormhole**.

**Sky Marshal Quill** (Bird-men) – *The Stormfeather*  
**Effect:** Place 1 FLEET from your reserves in a system that is adjacent to where your Flag Ship is located. Do this up to 3 times in up to 3 different systems (max. 1 fleet per system).

**Lord Granite** (Rock-men) – *The Unmoving*  
**Effect:** Replace 1 enemy FLEET from up to 2 systems adjacent to your Flag Ship with a FLEET from your reserves. Return the fleets you replaced to the reserves of their owners.

**The Many** (Weird Blobs) – *The Thousand-Minded*  
**Effect:** Move up to 3 FLEETS of the same opponent from the system where your Flag Ship is located to 1 or more adjacent systems.
