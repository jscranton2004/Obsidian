# Stellar Hegemony - Adapted Tactic Cards

**Last Updated:** 2026-05-28
**Source:** Rumble Nation Tactic Cards (mechanics preserved exactly)

**Important Rule:**
- “Adjacent” = can use either hyperspace lanes **or** wormholes
- Cards that specify “via hyperspace lanes” or “via wormhole” are restricted to that route only

---

## Movement Cards

**Scout**  
Move 1 of your FLEETS to an adjacent system.

**Hyperspace Invasion**  
Move 2 of your FLEETS from 1 system to an adjacent system **via hyperspace lanes**.

**Wormhole Invasion**  
Move 2 of your FLEETS from 1 system to an adjacent system **via wormhole**.

**Sub-Light Squadron**  
Move ½ of your FLEETS (rounded down) from 1 system to an adjacent system.

**Shadow Fleet**  
Move 1 of your FLEETS to any system where you already have FLEETS.

**Converge on the Core**  
Move any number of your FLEETS to the **Core System** from systems adjacent to it.

---

## Support & Disruption Cards

**Reinforce**  
Deploy 1 FLEET from your reserves into any system where you already have FLEETS.

**Insurrection**  
Return 1 enemy FLEET to its reserves and replace it with a FLEET from your reserves.

**Expel**  
Move 1 enemy FLEET from a system where you have FLEETS to an adjacent system.

**Seize**  
Move 1 enemy FLEET from any system to an adjacent system where you have FLEETS.

**False Flag**  
Move 1 of your FLEETS along with 2 enemy FLEETS to an adjacent system.

**Emergency Withdrawal**  
Return 2 of your FLEETS from any system(s) to your reserves.
