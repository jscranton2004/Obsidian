## Goal
Review the 6 factions and their leaders to identify any obvious balance issues before deeper playtesting.

## Scope (Medium)

- Look for any factions that appear significantly stronger or weaker
- Suggest small adjustments if clear imbalances are found

Before investing in extensive playtesting, it is useful to do a high-level balance pass on the leaders and factions to catch any obvious problems early.
