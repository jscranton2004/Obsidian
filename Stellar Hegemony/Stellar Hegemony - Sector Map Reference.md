# Stellar Hegemony - Sector Map Reference

**Source:** Rumble Nation board layout (11 sectors)

**Notes:**
- Original numbers refer to the gold-backed tokens on the physical board.
- Wormholes = special movement routes (equivalent to sea connections).
- Hyperspace = normal adjacency.
- Sectors 7 (Aether Reach) and 11 (The Shattered Rim) are intentionally isolated.
